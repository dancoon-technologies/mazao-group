# Troubleshooting FAQ

## Authentication

### "Session expired" appears frequently

- Verify token refresh endpoint health.
- Confirm device time is accurate.
- Re-authenticate and retest.

## Mobile Capture

### Cannot capture location

- Check app permission in device settings.
- Ensure GPS is enabled and retry outdoors if needed.

### Cannot capture photo

- Check camera permission and available storage.

## Visit Submission

### Validation error on submit

- Read field-level message and correct values.
- Confirm required fields for selected activities are filled.

### Visit not syncing

- Confirm network connectivity.
- Keep app open briefly after reconnect.
- Check backend availability.

## Schedules

### Proposal not visible to supervisor

- Confirm same department/region scope.
- Refresh list and verify status.

## Maintenance

### Unable to move incident status

- Confirm action is being done by supervisor role.
- Ensure GPS permission is available for verification steps.

## Tracking

### Team map not updating

- Verify officers are online and reporting.
- Confirm tracking endpoints are reachable.
